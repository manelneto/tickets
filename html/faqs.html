Dashboard Tickets FAQ
How can I submit a new ticket?

In order to submit a new Ticket, you need only to access the Tickets option in the left sidebar. After that, you will on the top right side a button that says "New Ticket". Just simply press on that button and fill the options that best suite your inquire.

How long will I have to wait for my ticket to me solved?

Each ticket has a resolution expectancy of up to 72 hours. Depending on each issue specifications, it may may occusionaly happen that its resolution may be quicker or even, take slight longer than initially expected. Nonetheless, you can always check the current status of your ticket and contact the agent responsible for it in the "Tickets" option on the left side-bar.

Where can I see the current status of my tickets?

You can check the current status of your tickets by accessing the "Tickets" option on the left side-bar. After selecting that option, you will see an option that allows you to check the current status, history and agent assigned to your ticket.
